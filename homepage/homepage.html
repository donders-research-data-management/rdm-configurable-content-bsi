Welcome to the BSI Repository

The Behavioural Science Institute (BSI) is a research institute of the Faculty of Social Sciences at the Radboud University. It was re-accredited as a research school by the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences (KNAW) in 2012. The Institute offers a two-year Research Master's (RM) programme in Behavioural Science, taught within the BSI Graduate School. In 2010 BSI received a highly selective official recognition as a graduate school by the Netherlands Organisation for Scientific Research (NWO).
